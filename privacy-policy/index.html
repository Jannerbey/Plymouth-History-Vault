Plymouth History Vault – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Plymouth History Vault (“the App”) is a community-driven historical archive created and operated in Plymouth, United Kingdom. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Uploads

When you upload an item (image, video, audio, PDF), we store:

  • The uploaded file
  • Title and description
  • Copyright details
  • Any names or dates you choose to include
  • Device identifier (not linked to your identity)

1.2 Moderation and Reporting

If you report an item, we store:

  • Your message
  • The ID of the item reported
  • Timestamp
  • Submitter’s email (until issue resolved, then discarded)

1.3 Technical Data

The App may collect non-personal technical data such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Error logs (for crash reporting)

2. What We Do NOT Collect & Store

We do not collect or store:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How Your Information Is Used

We use the information you provide to:

  • Display historical submissions within the App
  • Moderate uploaded content
  • Protect the App from misuse
  • Improve performance and reliability

We do not sell or share your personal data with anyone — ever.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud services. Access is restricted to the App owner and authorised moderators.

5. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Removal of any uploads you submitted
  • Removal of any data you provided through error reports or reports

Requests can be made through the App or via the support contact below.
